How Field Service Teams Reduce No-Shows and Reschedules

Every no-show is roughly $200-500 in lost revenue and crew time. Here's the operational workflow top performers use to cut no-show rate by 50-70%.

1. Confirm at booking

Two-way SMS confirmation at the moment of booking. ServiceTitan and Housecall Pro send this automatically; smaller-stack teams can use Podium.

ServiceTitan →Housecall Pro →Podium →

2. 24-hour reminder

Automated SMS + email day-before. Re-confirm appointment window. Offer easy reschedule link.

ServiceTitan →Housecall Pro →Jobber →

3. Day-of dispatch SMS

"Tech is on the way" SMS with tech name and photo. This single touch reduces no-shows by 30%+ in practice.

ServiceTitan →Housecall Pro →Podium →

4. Live GPS tracking

Customer sees the truck approaching, just like an Uber. ServiceTitan, FieldEdge, and Housecall Pro all support this.

ServiceTitan →FieldEdge →Housecall Pro →

5. Follow-up on no-shows

Automated SMS within 30 minutes asking what happened. About 1 in 3 reschedules vs being lost to a competitor.

Podium →ServiceTitan →

Takeaway

Steps 3 and 4 are the highest-leverage. Most teams implement step 2 first because it's easy — but step 3 moves the needle hardest.
