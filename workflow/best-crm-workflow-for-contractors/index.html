The Best CRM Workflow for Contractor Businesses

Generic CRM advice doesn't apply to contractors. Your sales process happens in the truck. Here's the workflow tailored to how trade businesses actually operate.

1. Capture every lead, every channel

Web form, phone, walk-in referrals, Google Maps, Yelp, Angi, Thumbtack — all into one queue. CallRail for call tracking, your CRM for everything else.

CallRail →Housecall Pro →Jobber →ServiceTitan →Angi Leads →Thumbtack →

2. Tag lead source on every record

Without source tagging, you can't calculate channel ROI. Most contractor CRMs have a one-click source field. Use it ruthlessly.

ServiceTitan →Jobber →Housecall Pro →

3. Auto-route to dispatcher

Inbound web lead → SMS to dispatcher within 60 seconds. Speed-to-lead is the #1 conversion lever in trade services.

ServiceTitan →Housecall Pro →Workiz →

4. Standardize the in-home quote

Tablet-based flat-rate pricebook. Same options shown to every customer. Conversion rate goes up; price erosion goes down.

ServiceTitan →Jobber →Housecall Pro →

5. Follow-up sequence on unwon quotes

Day 1, day 3, day 7, day 30 — automated SMS + email. About 15% of "lost" quotes will book within 30 days from this sequence alone.

ServiceTitan →Jobber →Podium →

6. Recurring service triggers

Annual maintenance reminders, water heater anniversaries, HVAC seasonal tune-ups. Set once, runs forever.

ServiceTitan →FieldEdge →Housecall Pro →

Takeaway

Most contractors get to step 2 and stall. The compounding returns happen at steps 5 and 6 — and they're the cheapest to implement.
